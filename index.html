D.A.R. Method

Data contains the answers to a multitude of questions considered essential for a business to achieve superior positions. Different hierarchical levels and sectors of a company have specific concerns and particularities. Therefore, it is unlikely that the same application or visualization serves everyone.

This is because excess or lack of information can both distract and hinder a user - or, worse, misinform them. Therefore, to ensure that the correct attention and information are extracted and applied, DexterBI's Business Intelligence team uses three different ways of exposing information:

Dashboards

Macro version of your company's information. A dashboard contains the strategic KPIs for decision making.

Analysis

Panels with more filters, areas and charts. It has greater interactivity, allowing greater exploration by the user.

Reporting

A reporting has a greater amount of information, containing specific data about the organization and tables.
